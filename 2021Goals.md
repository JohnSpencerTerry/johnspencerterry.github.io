# 2021 Goals

These are my goals for 2021. I think they’ll expand as time goes on so this list will definitely change. 

1.	Professional Goals 
    1.	Create a professional voice online and in person.
    2.	Find a role that allows for career growth and fulfillment.
2.	Financial Goals
    1.	Use a budget to control expenses.
    2.	Save money each month and focus on accruing wealth.
    3.	Create secondary income streams.
3.	Running Goals
    1.	Set PRs in 1 Mile, 5k, 10k, and Half Marathon
    2.	Stay consistent in training and increase load safely – build off 2020 successes. 
4.	Personality Goals
    1.	Be a nicer and more optimistic person.
    2.	Compartmentalize and be less stressed about uncontrollable things.
